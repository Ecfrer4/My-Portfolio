Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does a project take?
A: On average it takes us between 2-4 weeks to finish any given project.

Q: Is there a material fee?
A: Yes, the material fee is integrated in with the overall cost

Q: Do you make non-metal projects?
A: Yes, we make wood projects as well.

Q: Do you deliver finished projects?
A: For projects that are of 4x4 size we will deliver them if the customer is within 100 miles from our shop. If the customer is farther than that, then we will mail the project, but if it's bigger than 4x4 then we request that the customer picks up their project personally.

Q: Do you allow customers to watch your process or tour your facilities?
A: Because of safety reasons we only allow our personnel to go inside the shop areas.
